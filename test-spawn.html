🔬 Player Spawn Position Test Suite

ℹ️ About These Tests:
This test suite identifies edge cases and bugs in player starting positions during level restarts and checkpoint respawns. The tests simulate various maze configurations and spawn scenarios to catch issues where players spawn inside walls or at invalid positions.
⚠️ Known Issue:
Players have been observed spawning on maze wall edges during restarts, causing them to get stuck. These tests will help identify the root cause and validate fixes.
